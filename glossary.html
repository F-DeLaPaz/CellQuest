📚 Glossary of Terms

Explore key science and game terms used throughout your missions.

🧪 Science Terms

Mitosis
Cell division process where one cell splits into two identical daughter cells, essential for growth and healing.
iPSCs (Induced Pluripotent Stem Cells)
Stem cells engineered to become any type of cell via mitosis in the BioForge.
Phagocyte
A type of immune cell that engulfs and digests pathogens.
Keratinocyte
Skin cell type that rebuilds the barrier and helps seal wounds.
Neuron
Electrical signal-transmitting cells of the nervous system—not involved in healing skin.
Erythrocyte
Red blood cells that carry oxygen—not used in infection defense.

🎮 Game Terms

BioForge
The central lab where iPSCs are mitotically cloned into specialized cells.
CTS (Cellular Transport System)
The internal body-wide logistics system used to deliver new cells to where they’re needed.
Lymphatic Express
Fun name for how immune cells (like phagocytes) travel—real-world route: lymphatic vessels.
Slipstream EpiRail
Game term for local delivery of keratinocytes via epithelial tissue.
